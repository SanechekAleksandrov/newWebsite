My food preferences

Being foodie is not that simple. Especially here in Europe. Variety of different dishes comes from every corner.

Here are some of my favourite cuisines:

Turkish Cuisine: Hospitality in Every Bite

The aroma of spices, the crunch of baklava, and juicy kebabs turn every meal into a celebration. Turkish cuisine is a ritual of sharing, warmth, and enjoying life at a slower pace.

Swiss Cuisine: Comfort on a Plate

Cheese fondue, fine chocolate, and hearty alpine dishes reflect the calm and quality of Swiss living. This cuisine is all about simplicity, warmth, and flavor.

Japanese Cuisine: Art in Every Detail

Sushi, ramen, and seasonal dishes reflect harmony with nature and a deep sense of aesthetics. Japanese food is all about balance, freshness, and elegant presentation.

Slavic Cuisine: Hearty and Soulful

Borscht, dumplings, and fermented vegetables bring the comfort of home. Slavic cuisine is rich, filling, and rooted in tradition and warmth.
